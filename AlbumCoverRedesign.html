Album Cover Redesign

I wanted to try my hand at redesigning an album from my favorite band at the time, Imagine Dragons. With this project, I wanted to put some restrictions on myself to see what i could do.
1. The new design should keep the original in mind.
2. Any stock photos I use, had to be taken by me.
3. I only had a few days to complete it.

Due to the short time limit I knew that I did not have much time to take photos, So i dived into my photography collection. After finding six photos that I liked to work with, I hopped into photoshop and started messing around with them. Over several iterations of doing this, I came upon a layout that I was happy with and started making a mockup of my new design to see how it might look as a product.
