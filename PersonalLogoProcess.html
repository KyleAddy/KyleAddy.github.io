Personal Logo Design and Process

For my personal logo i knew that I had three main points I wanted to hit.
1. Logo needs to quickly tell the viewer what it was for.
2. I wanted the logo to be minimalistic as that is something I enjoy
3. The logo needed to easily fit on a business card.

After researching designs of other logos, I started to sketch out some designs. I made several versions of my favorite sketch, decided on a font and color scheme and then put them all together to make the final Product. A minimalistic, rectangular logo that incorporates the first letter of my first and last name.
